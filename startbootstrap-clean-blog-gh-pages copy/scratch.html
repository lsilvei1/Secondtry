My hometown

I was born in the United States but moved to Brazil when I was 8 months old. I lived in the city of Criciuma and had a wonderful childhood. I remember playing games with my friends until late at night. Life was a lot different back then and I had no phones and barely any technology. I had dear friends of whom I will never forget. I moved back to the United States at 15 years of age and embarked on a new adventure.
